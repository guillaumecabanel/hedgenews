Colors

Rule: Only use colors defined via variables in your variables.scss stylesheet.

There's a lot of website to help you setting up your color palette. Coolors.co is one of them !

Usually 5 colors is "way enough" to cover background-color, font-colors, brand and brand secondary colors.

White background is always a safe choice !

Layout

Which layouts do you plan to use accross the app ?

Which global layout positionning technique do you plan on using ? bootsrap-grid, flex ?

Rule: Use 1 responsive width size for your "standard" form (login, signup, edit, etc.) and use it with consistance accross the app.

Rule: Absolute/Relative positionning is for small details ! Never use it to design a whole card of list items.
